## Key Decisions

- The product wedge is automatic capture plus real-time interception.
- The narrative is aimed at decision makers, not implementation readers.
- Benchmarks and dogfood data are used as auditable evidence, with token overhead stated as a current trade-off.
- The roadmap presents delivered scope separately from planned team and multi-tool capabilities.
